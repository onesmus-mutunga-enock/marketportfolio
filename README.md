# Romin Mansuri - Supply Chain & Business Portfolio

Academic portfolio for DESN3205 meeting requirements for structured presentation of qualifications and practical experience.

## Profile
Retail & Supply Chain Professional transitioning to finance, accounting, and business roles. Postgraduate in Supply Chain Management from Bow Valley College.

## Features
- ✅ Fully responsive Tailwind CSS design
- ✅ Vanilla JavaScript data-driven content
- ✅ Interactive case study modals (Objective-Problem-Strategy-Execution-Outcome-Learning)
- ✅ Dynamic skills/testimonials/timeline rendering
- ✅ CV download functionality
- ✅ Professional/academic tone
- ✅ Lightweight static site (GitHub Pages ready)

## Sections
1. **Introduction** - Professional qualifications overview
2. **Resume** - Education & Loblaw experience with quantified achievements
3. **Marketing Artifacts** - Academic marketing analysis work
4. **Case Studies** - 3 analytical SCM-relevant studies
5. **Skills** - Categorized competencies w/ proficiency levels
6. **Reflection** - Project analysis entries
7. **Development** - Professional timeline
